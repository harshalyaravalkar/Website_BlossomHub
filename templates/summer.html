Summer Flowers

Common Name: French Marigold
Scientific Name: Tagetes patula
Typically, french marigold blooms from early spring or summer to fall (February-September).

Common Name: Sunflower
Scientific name: Helianthus annuus
The plant flowers in summer. What is often called the "flower" of the sunflower is actually a "flower head" (pseudanthium), 7.5–12.5 centimetres (3–5 in) wide,[4] of numerous small individual five-petaled flowers ("florets").

Common Name: Zinnia
Scientific Name: Chrysogonum peruvianum
Zinnias flower in India in the late summer. They can be sown all year, but are mainly grown in February-March and October-December.

Common Name: Cosmos
Scientific Name: Cosmos bipinnatus
Cosmos flowers bloom from early summer until frost. They are annual plants that thrive in warm and dry climates. They are easy to grow from seed and can even survive in poor soil conditions.
